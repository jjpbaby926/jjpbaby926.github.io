Born and bred in San Francisco, West Coast Pro Wrestling is proud to represent Northern California on the global stage.

With a deep respect for the Bay Area’s rich professional wrestling history and a commitment to elevating the next generation, West Coast Pro Wrestling brings together the most elite independent wrestlers from around the country to forge a new legacy in the heart of the bay.

The academy blends passion with discipline — offering hands-on training, in-ring fundamentals, and mentorship from respected professionals.

If you're lacing up your boots for the first time or refining your skills at a semi-pro level, the Academy is built to develop wrestlers at every stage of their journey.
