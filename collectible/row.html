Reality of Wrestling





Base Location:Houston texas (2005)




Champions:


Men's World: Danny Limelight

Women's World: "Hollywood Haley J"


Texas Champion: Max Castellanos



Owner Booker T started the promotion to give professional wrestlers the opportunity to properly train,
as well as to keep the legacy of Paul Boesch's Houston Wrestling alive.


On March 9, 2012, after seven years as the Pro Wrestling Alliance, the promotion was re-launched as Reality of Wrestling (ROW)
